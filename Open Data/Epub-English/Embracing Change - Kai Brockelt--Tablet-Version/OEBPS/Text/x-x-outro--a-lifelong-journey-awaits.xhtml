A lifelong journey awaits

Congratulations!

You made it to the end of this book. This is great! From now on, you are equipped to shape your healthy future. You can act wisely on the journey ahead with all the knowledge gained.

Why is this awesome?

You have picked up deep nutritional knowledge that helps you make intelligent decisions. With your honed understanding of nutrition and flavor creation, you can craft a diet style that fits your life.

The long road behind

Over the last 30 chapters, you learned everything needed to distinguish healthy eating patterns from lousy advice and green-labeled products that claim to be more than they are.

You developed a deep understanding about the metabolism of macronutrients like carbs, fat, and protein, and you know how to make good choices that can fuel your body and mind in the long run.

You learned about whole food groups, their importance, and many tricks to integrate them into your daily life.

You know the essential nutrients for health and have the tools to make the most out of what real food offers.

You can read and understand what’s written on the product's nutrition labels and can quickly scan for good and bad signals.

You learned concepts around taste, aroma, texture, and bite, and you know how to design complete taste and a wholesome experience.

You encountered ways to cook soups, bowls, stews, finger food, pasta dishes, and a combined holiday menu. You have plenty of inspiration for changing and bending these dishes in your desired direction.

You developed a deep understanding of how diet and lifestyle relate to the probability of developing the most common non-communicable diseases and know how to counteract the odds.

The journey ahead

With all this, you are equipped to shape your healthy life. It is up to you whether you want to follow a dietary pattern like Keto, Mediterranean, Vegetarian, or Vegan. All these patterns can be designed to conform to the consent for a healthy diet.

I recommend not to follow any pattern word by word. Take what best matches you from all the trends, styles, or recommendations you might encounter, and cherry-pick to your liking. It’s your life, so it’s your choice.

Make it truly yours by listening to what you need and want. Your best choice is never advised from the outside, but you find your answers in your heart and mind.

Continue listening to new ideas, but don’t take everything for granted. If you hear a new claim, ask yourself “why” and “how” this claim could fit into the big picture. And then decide if this claim is for you.

And again, please don’t fall for perfectionism. Take your time to learn. Allow yourself the flexibility that’s needed to move forward. Like driving, true mastery comes with practice. Make mistakes and grow on them. Stay curious and creative. Be kind. Embrace this positive mindset, and you will move mountains.

Let’s make the world a little better!

I have written this book with a clear purpose: to tackle problems our modern society creates. The weakest part of society often pays the price for chasing money and corporate greed. Big companies generate profit, often neglecting their negative impact on the individual's life.

I firmly believe everybody deserves to live a healthy and happy life following their interests and ideas. And that the safest way out of the jungle of misinformation is education and enablement. Awareness crafts resilience, and knowledge is a sharp sword to cut old ropes.

This battle can only be won with patience, empathy, and authenticity. If you also think like this, please consider kindness if facing tough discussions about diet and health. Share clarity and good information instead of attacking wrong ideas face to face. When you encounter challenging discussions full of attacks, the worst answer to fire is fire. It should never be “your ideas against my ideas” but a kind offer from an open hand reaching out. It’s up to the others to take it or leave it.

Planting the right ideas can break foul biases. Know how you can affect your family, friends, and peers.
